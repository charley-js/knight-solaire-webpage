Knight Solaire of Astora

Who is Solaire?

Knight Solaire of Astora is a charcter from the video game Dark Souls, he is a Warrior of Sunlight and is a fan favourite due to his jolly attitude and famous line of dialogue 'Praise the Sun'.

Solaire is on a quest to find 'his very own sun' , however it remains a mystery of what this actually is. He is an exceptionally powerful skilled knight, being trained in armed combat and sunlight miracles.

There are two possible endings for Solaire, the majority of the time the Knight encounters a parasite known as a Sunlight Maggot which he becomes obsessed with and ultimately goes insane due to its emition of a blinding light. The other ending , Solaire helps the protagonist 'Link the Fire', which can be implied he has then found his sun.

Solaire does not directly appear in the other Souls games however his legacy is often referred too, with his armour, equipment, holy symbols and miracles referenced throughout.

Dialogue

Knight Solaire is renowned by fans due to his friendly demeanor and his in-game dialogue, some of his most favourable quotes are as follows:

  • "I am Solaire of Astora, an adherent to the Lord of Sunlight. Now that I am Undead, I have come to this great land, the birthplace of Lord Gwyn, to seek my very own sun!"
  • "The way I see it, our fates appear to be intertwined. In a land brimming with Hollows, could that really be mere chance? So, what do you say? Why not help one another on this lonely journey?"
  • "Praise the sun!"

Encounters

Solaire is encountered numerous times throughout the protagonists journey, he can be found in the following locations:

  1. Undead Burg
  2. Sunlight Altar
  3. Anor Londo
  4. Demon Ruins
  5. Lost Izalith

Easter Eggs

  • A reference to Solaire can be found in Borderlands 2, where a character called Solitaire is sitting next to a bonfire.
  • Solaire's armour and shield can be seen in a show called Turbo Fast, Episode 17
  • A reference to Solaire can be found in World of Warcraft, where a character called Soulaire of Andorhal will give the player a bonfire toy.
  • Solaire is referenced in Old School Runescape, where a Knight of Varlamore found in Castle Kourend is wearing his exact armour
